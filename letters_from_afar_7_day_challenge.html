Letters from Afar

7-Day Challenge

Day 1: The First Light

Emotion Puzzle:

“The dawn broke gently, with a promise of new beginnings. One emotion was bright, one was soft, and one was hidden.”

Choose 3 emotions: Joy, Longing, Clarity, Wonder, Calm, Curiosity

Letter Prompt:

Write a short letter using these words: “Awake,” “Edge,” and “Whisper.”

Token Trade:

Current tokens: 2 × Curiosity, 1 × Joy, 1 × Longing

Trade options: 2 Curiosity for 1 Wonder, 1 Joy + 1 Longing for 1 Clarity, or keep your tokens.

Reflection: “What did you notice today that you might have missed yesterday?”
